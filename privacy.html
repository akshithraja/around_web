Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

AroundBee ("AroundBee," "we," "us," or "our") is operated by AROUNDBEE PRIVATE LIMITED. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the AroundBee mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

  • Account information — we sign you in using your phone number with one-time password (OTP) verification (via Firebase Authentication). We collect your phone number, along with the name and profile details you add.
  • Location data — with your permission, we collect precise location to tag where each Trail stop was created and to show you relevant nearby Trails. You can disable location access at any time in your device settings.
  • Photos and videos — the media you capture or select to add to your Trails.
  • Messages and user content — direct messages you send to other users, along with Trail descriptions, comments, and other content you create in the App.
  • Usage and analytics data — we use PostHog to understand how the App is used (for example, screens viewed and features used) so we can improve it. This may include device and interaction data.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the App and its features;
  • To tag and display the location of your Trail stops;
  • To enable messaging and sharing between users;
  • To improve and personalize your experience;
  • To communicate with you about your account or the pilot; and
  • To keep the App secure and prevent misuse.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these cases:

  • With other users, when you choose to — for example, content in public Trails or messages you send.
  • With service providers who process data on our behalf:
    • Google Firebase — authentication, database, storage, and notifications;
    • Google Maps Platform — maps and location features;
    • PostHog — product analytics.
  • When required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users and AroundBee.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App. When you delete your account, we delete or anonymize your personal information, except where we are required to retain it by law.

Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and update your profile information directly in the App;
  • Control location and photo permissions in your device settings;
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data by emailing us at aroundbeedev@gmail.com.

Security

We use industry-standard measures, including encryption in transit, to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and revise the "Last updated" date above. Significant changes may also be communicated through the App.
